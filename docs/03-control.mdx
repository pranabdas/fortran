Operators | Meaning
:-------: | :-----------------------
.lt.	  | less than
.gt.	  | greater than
.le.	  | less than or equal to
.ge.	  | greater than or equal to
.eq.	  | equals
.ne.	  | not equal to
.and.	  | and
.or.	  | or
.not.	  | not
.xor.	  | "exclusive" or
.eqv.	  | equivalent
.neqv.    | not equivalent

F90 introduced following simplified notation for the relational operators:

Operators | Meaning
:-------: | :-----------------------
&lt;   	  | less than
&gt;      | greater than
&lt;=     | less than or equal to
&gt;=     | greater than or equal to
==        | equal to
/=        | not equal to
